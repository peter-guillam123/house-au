Senate Estimates.

Twice-yearly hearings where senators question departmental officials over budget allocations — the surface where most of the substantive accountability stories surface (robodebt and sports rorts both came up here first). Will filter by round (Budget / Supplementary / Additional), portfolio, department and witness. The shape that doesn’t exist in House UK because there’s no UK equivalent.
